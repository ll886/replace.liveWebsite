About Us

Our mission is to bring easy streaming to college students around the country.

COLLEGE STREAMING CHANGED FOR THE BETTER? Local genius group of Philadelphian boys creates Chromecast alternative that works over college WiFi. The methods they used challenge industrial standard by exploring experimental and new technologies such as Flutter and Dart to create a single codebase to work across any device! The software is available for purchase from replacing .live/box for $29.99. Our idea is to create a progressive web app to revolutionize college streaming. A Chromecast alternative using NodeJS, Flutter, and Dart, will allow users to casually stream live video to their TV over campus WiFi. The website will be always running on a small Internet of Things device such as a Raspberry Pi. It will present users with a QR code to scan via their phones which will open a Flutter application to control the TV.
